## Step 1: Enable "Apps from Unknown Sources"

Before you can install any IPTV app, you need to prepare your Firestick:

1. Go to **Settings** on your Firestick home screen.
2. Select **My Fire TV**.
3. Choose **Developer Options**. (If you don't see this, go to 'About' and click on your Fire TV Stick name 7 times).
4. Turn on **Install Unknown Apps**.

## Step 2: Install the Downloader App

The **Downloader** app is the most reliable tool for side-loading IPTV applications:

1. Search for "Downloader" in the Firestick App Store.
2. Download and open the app.
3. Grant the requested permissions.
